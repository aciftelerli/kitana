Strategic

Market Entry Barriers

01
Incumbent System Fatigue
"The Good Enough Competitor"

Existing tools use primitive, spreadsheet-like logic. Despite being cumbersome, they create a dangerous "I already have a solution" mindset, leading to change resistance.

02
SME Budget & Friction
"The SaaS Subscription Overload"

SMEs are overwhelmed by multiple SaaS subscriptions and reluctant to onboard yet another new system due to limited operational budgets.

03
Trust & Data Security
"The Status Quo Bias"

Customers hesitate to entrust a new vendor with all their financial and supply chain data. Many are culturally used to relying on their main POS provider for everything.

Mitigation Strategy

1

The Intelligence Layer

We prove we are 10x better by offering features current tools lack: AI-powered forecasting, real-time stock alerts, and suggested profit multiplier changes. We don't just record the past — we predict the future.

Predictive vs. Descriptive
2

Integration-First Entry

Zero setup friction. No new hardware or lengthy migration. Achieve full integration with existing POS systems (Adisyo, Simpra) and offer a compelling, low-cost entry tier promising tangible ROI.

Zero Setup Friction
3

Partner Endorsement

Strategic partnerships and official integrations with major local POS providers position Kitana as a "Partner-Endorsed Integration," leveraging the existing trust between operators and their primary system.

Build Trust via POS Partners
